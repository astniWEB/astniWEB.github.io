This EP is a collection of covers I've made in the past that was released just for fun.

Commentary:

No. Track Name Commentary
1 Go Straight (Streets of Rage 2) this is one of the first good covers i made, probably back in 2022, just slightly tweaked. honestly one of my favorites to go back to, still pretty happy with it.

3 Lying Coldly (Ace Attorney: Investigations) not too bad but nothing special. with how good of a song this is i would've liked to make something better, but it's fine for what it is i guess. maybe some effects would've helped things. i do want to make more ace attorney covers though.
4 Title Theme (Action 52) honestly one of my favorites from the EP, which is surprising considering it's action 52. this one wasn't too complicated either, the original was already pretty good so i just put my own spin on it.
5 Cheetahmen (Action 52) say what you will about this game but the music kinda bangs. which is too bad because this is the worst cover in the EP. this is straight ass, i think i was just tired of making these covers.
